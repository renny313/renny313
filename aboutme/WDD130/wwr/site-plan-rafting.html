WHITEWATER RAFTING TRIP WITH GREENSPRING

GreenSpring helped pioneer whitewater rafting in the Western Africa. And since we first got started in 2023, we have guided more than 200,000 guests on rivers all over the world. Now, with more than 65 unique itineraries, we offer travelers more whitewater rafting trips than any other outfitter in Africa. But it’s not just about how long we’ve been in business, or the number of trips we offer. It’s really about the GreenSpring experience. No matter what type of river adventure you are seeking, Whether it’s a quick, one-day whitewater rafting trip, a week-long river trip, or an epic 10-day rafting expedition, we can guarantee a trip with GreenSpring will end up being one of your best vacations ever. Because, let’s be honest, we’re not talking about a trip to Disneyland here. River trips are the ultimate way to escape the grind, disconnect and spend quality time with friends or loved ones, or even make new lifelong friends. Not only that, there is no better way to experience some of the most scenic and remote wilderness areas in the world than from a raft, dory, inflatable kayak or stand up paddleboard. And trust us, we know choosing the right rafting trip can be a bit overwhelming, so don’t hesitate to give us a call at 070-4035-9036. Our dedicated year-round office staff and team of adventure consultants have hundreds of trips among us, so we know we can match you with the perfect trip and answer any and every question you may have leading up to your adventure.
We are conveniently located an hour or less from Lekki Phase 1, Lagos Island, Nigeria.
